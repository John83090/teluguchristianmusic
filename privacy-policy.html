Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 13, 2026

This policy applies to the Telugu Christian Music Android app and the Keerthana iOS app, both published by John Paul (teluguchristianmusic.com).

1. Information We Collect

Anonymous Device Identifier

We generate a random UUID the first time you open the app and store it locally on your device. This identifier is anonymous — it is never linked to your name, email address, or any account. It is used solely to aggregate usage statistics.

Usage Analytics

We automatically log the following events to our Firebase Firestore database:

  • app_open — recorded each time the app is launched
  • song_play — the song ID and title when playback starts

Each event also includes your device model, OS version, app version, and approximate region (locale/country code). No precise location data is collected.

Song Requests

When you voluntarily submit a Song Request through the in-app form, we collect the information you provide: YouTube link, song name, artist name, and any notes. This data is stored in our Cloudflare Worker and Firebase Firestore solely to process your request.

Downloaded Songs

Song files you choose to download are stored locally on your device. The list of downloaded song IDs and metadata is stored only on your device; we do not upload your download history to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To understand which songs and features are most popular so we can improve the app
  • To process song requests you submit
  • To enforce the daily free-download quota and reward-ad flow
  • To send optional worship-related push notifications (you can disable these in your device settings)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Analytics, Cloud Messaging) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob — serves banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — processes premium subscription payments — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudflare Workers — stores song request submissions — cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

4. Data Retention

Analytics events are retained in Firestore for up to 12 months, after which older records are deleted. Song request data is retained until manually removed by the app administrator. Downloaded song files remain on your device until you delete them from within the app or uninstall the app; they are automatically removed 30 days after download.

5. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at the address below and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Disable push notifications from your device's notification settings
  • Opt out of personalised ads through your device's ad settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising)
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you by emailing us

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Firebase Firestore data is protected by Google's security infrastructure. We do not store any passwords or payment card details — payments are handled entirely by Google Play Billing.

8. Terms of Use

By using this app you agree to the following terms:

  • All songs in the app are community recordings by independent worship artists. They are not official commercial releases and are provided for personal, non-commercial worship use only.
  • You may not reproduce, redistribute, or use any audio content for commercial purposes.
  • The app may display advertisements. You agree not to click ads artificially or attempt to circumvent the ad system.
  • Misuse of the Song Request feature (spam, abusive content) may result in your requests being ignored.
  • We reserve the right to modify or discontinue the service at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitute acceptance of the new policy.
